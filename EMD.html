Exercise 1 Solution 10 pts

1. Z-transform 1.25 pts

\[ Z\{2u[n]\}=\frac{2}{1-z^{-1}} \] \[ Z\{-(0.5)^nu[n]\}=-\frac{1}{1-0.5z^{-1}} \] \[ Z\{3\delta[n-2]\}=3z^{-2} \] \[ X(z)=\frac{2}{1-z^{-1}}-\frac{1}{1-0.5z^{-1}}+3z^{-2} \]

2. Region of convergence 1 pt

The poles are \(z=1\) and \(z=0.5\). Since the signal is right-sided: \[ ROC:\ |z|>1 \]

3. Inverse Z-transform 1.25 pts

\[ \frac{2z}{z-0.4}=\frac{2}{1-0.4z^{-1}} \Rightarrow 2(0.4)^nu[n] \] \[ \frac{z}{z-1}=\frac{1}{1-z^{-1}} \Rightarrow u[n] \] \[ x[n]=2(0.4)^nu[n]+u[n] \]

4. Recurrence equation 1.75 pts

\[ (1-1.1z^{-1}+0.32z^{-2})Y(z) = (0.2+0.1z^{-1})X(z) \] \[ y[n]-1.1y[n-1]+0.32y[n-2]=0.2x[n]+0.1x[n-1] \] \[ y[n]=1.1y[n-1]-0.32y[n-2]+0.2x[n]+0.1x[n-1] \]

6. Drawing and Jury stability 3 pts

\[ D(z)=z^2-1.1z+0.32 \] \[ D(1)=1-1.1+0.32=0.22>0 \] \[ D(-1)=1+1.1+0.32=2.42>0 \] \[ |0.32|<1 \] Therefore, the system is stable.

Exercise 2 Solution 10 pts

1. Closed-loop block diagram 1 pt

Unity negative feedback: \[ R(z)\rightarrow \oplus \rightarrow C(z)\rightarrow G(z)\rightarrow Y(z) \] with \(Y(z)\) fed back negatively.

2. Open-loop function 1.5 pts

\[ C(z)=1+\frac{0.3}{z-1} = \frac{z-0.7}{z-1} \] \[ L(z)=\frac{z-0.7}{z-1}\cdot\frac{0.4z}{z-0.6} = \frac{0.4z(z-0.7)}{(z-1)(z-0.6)} \]

3. Closed-loop transfer function 2 pts

\[ F(z)=\frac{L(z)}{1+L(z)} \] \[ F(z)= \frac{0.4z(z-0.7)} {(z-1)(z-0.6)+0.4z(z-0.7)} \] \[ F(z)= \frac{0.4z^2-0.28z} {1.4z^2-1.88z+0.6} \]

4. Recurrence equation 1.5 pts

\[ (1.4z^2-1.88z+0.6)Y(z) = (0.4z^2-0.28z)R(z) \] \[ 1.4y[n]-1.88y[n-1]+0.6y[n-2] = 0.4r[n]-0.28r[n-1] \]
