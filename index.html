Important Instructions for Students

Official examination rules · Department of Electrical Engineering · Academic Year 2025 / 2026
Read Carefully

Examination Rules

Any violation leads to disciplinary action.
Please read all instructions carefully. Any violation will lead to immediate referral to the disciplinary committee.

Arrival Time

Students must arrive at least 10 minutes before the start of the exam.

Exam Duration

The duration of each examination is 120 minutes.

Late Entry

Entry is strictly forbidden after 30 minutes from the beginning of the exam.

No Early Exit

Students are not allowed to leave before 30 minutes from the start of the exam.

Mobile Phones

The use of mobile phones is strictly prohibited. Phones must not be used or placed on the table.

No Sharing Materials

Sharing or exchanging pens, calculators, or any other materials is strictly forbidden.

Respect Invigilators

Students must respect invigilators and follow all instructions during the examination.

Disciplinary Action

Any violation of the examination rules will lead to disciplinary action.

Good Luck

Prepare with Confidence

Study with focus, manage your time, arrive early, and respect all examination instructions. The Department of Electrical Engineering wishes success to all students.
