Why this digital timetable is useful

This interface does not replace the official administration. It presents the approved timetable in a clearer way for students, reduces confusion, highlights the nearest examination, and allows teachers and students to print a clean PDF version at any time.

Important Instructions for Students

Student Card Required
Students must bring their student card to every examination.
Arrive Early
Students must arrive at least 10 minutes before the exam starts.
No Late Entry
Entry is strictly forbidden after 30 minutes from the beginning.
No Early Exit
No student may leave before 30 minutes from the start.
Mobile Phones
Phones are strictly prohibited and must not be placed on the table.
No Sharing Materials
Sharing pens, calculators, rulers, or any material is strictly forbidden.
